Upcoming Events at Mont-Tremblant

Mont-Tremblant has new events almost every weekend for the whole family to participate in.
Here are the two next upcoming events!


Elevation


Jan. 31,2019-Feb. 3,2019



-From January 31 to February 3, Tremblant hosts Elevation, a new LGBTQ event. Get ready to ski and snowboard all day and enjoy lively apres-ski and festive evenings with renowned DJs.

Freestyle Air Nation


Feb. 2,2019-Feb. 3,2019



-Following in the foot steps of last year’s success, Freestyle Air Nation presented by Burton and Mazda is back at Tremblant for a second year. It’s a great opportunity to come watch and cheer on Canada’s top Slopestyle athletes who will be competing for the Air Nation Tour champion title. Athletes will compete on a course composed of three rails and two jumps in a best of two format for qualifications and finals. Fans will have access to view the competition in designated areas. An event not to be missed and to add at your winter schedule!

161 Chemin de la Falaise, QC
