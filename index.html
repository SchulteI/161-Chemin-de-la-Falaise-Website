About Our Condo

Welcome to our cozy condo on 161 chemin de la falaise, located in the town of Mont Tremblant, less than 10 minutes from the mountain.


The town of Mont Tremblant is situated on Lac Mercier, which during the summer provides a beutiful spot for swimming and boating activitys. Along with a large park, there are plenty of hiking trails, mountain biking and ziplining to do at Mont Tremblant. There are plenty of restaurents, and ski and bike shops to explore in the town, as well as the Mont Tremblant Circuit and golf courses. In the winter there you can either down hill ski or snowboard at Mont Tremblant, discover the abundance of cross country ski trails, or take in the breathtaking view from the top of the Mountain.


Our two bedroom condo sleeps 7 including the couch bed, is situated in a quiet, four condo complex, with a spacious porch and barbecue as well as a private driveway. There is also a storage locker for skis and skates. The condo is kid friendly, however, pets, smoking and partys are not allowed. There is a two day minimum required per stay and a 5 day cancellation policey where you may cancel your booking and get a full refund minus the service fee.


Enjoy your stay at 161 chemin de la falaise and discover everything that the Mont Tremblant area has to offer!
